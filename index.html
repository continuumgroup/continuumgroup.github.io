Continuum Group began at the 2013 National Day of Civic Hacking in Saint Louis, Missouri with a simple idea.

Empower the homeless to better their lives using technology

This idea attracted a group of people at the hackathon to get together for a weekend and start something great. That group, which would eventually become the Continuum Group, conceived and built a prototype of an automated telephone system that could enhance the existing shelter reservation systetm in Saint Louis, reducing wait times and staffing costs, while being expanded to shelters nation-wide.
